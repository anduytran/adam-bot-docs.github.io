# Privacy Policy for Adam Bot
Last updated: May 4, 2026

**1. Data Collection**
Adam Bot only accesses messages in Google Chat spaces where it is explicitly @mentioned or triggered via a slash command.

**2. Data Usage**
Collected data is used solely to generate project timelines and summaries as requested by the user.

**3. Data Storage**
We do not permanently store your chat logs. Data is processed in real-time and cleared after the workflow execution completes.

**4. Third-Party Sharing**
Your data is never sold or shared with third-party advertisers. Data is processed via raia/n8n infrastructures as part of your project environment.
